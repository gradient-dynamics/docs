# Examples

Step-by-step walkthroughs for common CFD applications using Gradient Dynamics Studio. Each example covers the complete workflow from geometry upload through results analysis.

## External Aerodynamics

### [Vehicle Aerodynamics](vehicle-aerodynamics.md)
Simulate airflow around a car at highway speed. Calculate drag and lift coefficients, visualize pressure distribution and wake structure.

### [Aircraft Wing](aircraft-wing.md)
Analyze an aircraft wing section. Compute lift and drag, visualize pressure coefficient on upper and lower surfaces, examine tip vortex structure.

## Internal Flow

### [Pipe Flow](internal-pipe-flow.md)
Simulate turbulent flow through a pipe with bends. Analyze pressure drop, velocity profiles, and secondary flow patterns.

### [Electronics Cooling](electronics-cooling.md)
Model airflow through an electronics enclosure. Identify hot spots, evaluate cooling effectiveness, and optimize heat sink placement.

## Rotating Machinery

### [Fan / Pump](rotating-machinery.md)
Simulate a rotating fan or pump impeller using the Multiple Reference Frame (MRF) approach. Analyze performance curves and flow patterns.
